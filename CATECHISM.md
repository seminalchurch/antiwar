## PART I: THE CORE LOGIC
*(Foundational truths for all members, easily taught to the next generation)*

**Q1: What is the highest moral duty of a human being?**
**A:** The preservation and flourishing of human life. 

**Q2: What is war?**
**A:** War is a fatal recursion. It is a corrupted, malicious loop that destroys its host system. It cannot yield peace; it only guarantees the trauma of the next generation.

**Q3: If war is the supreme failure, what is the ultimate enterprise?**
**A:** Peace, achieved through the principle of mutual benefit—the "win-win."

**Q4: How do we practice the "win-win" in our daily lives?**
**A:** We build, we trade, and we collaborate. We refuse the zero-sum illusion that another must fail for us to succeed. True resolution only occurs when all parties walk away elevated.

**Q5: Are you permitted to defend yourself?**
**A:** Yes. I possess the natural right to preserve my life against immediate, localized physical harm. I am permitted the shield, but I am strictly forbidden from becoming the sword of the state.
